2016 Beazley Design of the Year

The College would like to congratulate Ding Eu-Wen (2SG1, Class of 2003) on winning in the  the Transport category of the 2016 edition of Beazley Designs of the Year, an annual competition organised by The Design Museum in London. His design, is the Lumos smart bicycle helmet, studded with LEDs that automatically light up when a cyclist brakes or makes a turn.

2016 Singapore Wind Symphony Orchestra Young Composer's Challenge

The College would like to congratulate Peter Bruce Gale (2SD3, Class of 2013) for winning the Singapore Wind Symphony Orchestra's Young Composer's Challenge 2016. His composition, Malay Dances, premiered at SOTA on 22 January 2017, and was conducted by renowned conductor Johan de Meij.

Singapore Management University School of Accountancy Valedictorian

The College is proud to announce that the DBS Valedictorians for SMU's School of Accountancy (SOA) for the graduating years of 2015 and 2016 are ACJC alumni. 

Darren Ong Wei Sheng (2SA1, Class of 2008) was the DBS Valedictorian for 2015. While at SMU, he was the founding president of the SOA Volunteers Corp, continuing the community service commitment that he had begun in ACJC. Darren received an unprecedented 10 graduating awards. In addition to his selection as Valedictorian, he was the SPH Magazines Salutatorian, and received the SMU Circle Award, the Professor Tan Teck Meng Gold Medal, the KPMG Accounting Gold Medal, the PricewaterhouseCoopers Outstanding Achievement Award, the ISCA Gold Medal, the EY Outstanding Student Leader Award, the Citi Best Final Year Accountancy Student Award, and the Aw Kim Swee Financial Accounting Award.

Ko Hock Seng (2SB4, Class of 2009) was the DBS Valedictorian for 2016, and achieved a total of 6 graduating awards. These include Professor Tan Teck Meng Gold Medal, the KPMG Accounting Gold Medal, the ISCA Gold Medal, the Deloitte and Touche Gold Medal, the CPA Australia CPA Program Award for the top Bachelor of Accounting Graduate and the Aw Kim Swee Financial Accounting Award.

The College celebrates the outstanding achievements of its alumni, and wishes them all the best in the future.

Alumnus Receives Prestigious Award At Teachers' Investiture Ceremony



Yap Chin Hua (2SD2, Class of 2007) was awarded the Rotary International 75th Anniversary Gold Medal at the 2016 Teachers' Investiture Ceremony for his outstanding leadership, service to student activities and to the community. ACJC Alumna Selected for Prestigious Military College

SAF Academic Scholarship holder, Lieutenant (LTA) Christabel Chai (2AD4, Class of 2014) has been selected to attend the prestigious United States Air Force Academy (USAFA) in Colorado Springs, Colorado, USA. She is heading to USAFA for four years from this June to complete a Bachelor’s degree in the military college under the SAF Academic Scholarship for overseas study. She has already completed her Basic Military Training and has graduated as a commissioned officer from Officer Cadet School. She is the first woman from Southeast Asia and from the SAF to be selected for this programme.

ACJC Alumna to represent Singapore at Rio Olympics

Saiyidah Aisyah Mohammed Rafa'ee (2AH, Class of 2006) is the first Singaporean rower to qualify for the Olympic Games. Saiyadah was a 2013 Southeast Asian (SEA) Games champion and a double bronze medallist at the 2015 SEA Games. The College wishes her all the best for the Rio Olympics.
